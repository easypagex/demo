My Story

Where fine art meets beauty

I'm Anna Lysak — a Madrid-based makeup artist with an Art Academy degree and over eight years of hands-on experience transforming clients across Europe.

My academic background in fine arts means I approach every face as a canvas: studying structure, light, and proportion before a single brush touches the skin. That foundation, combined with hundreds of real-world weddings and events, is what sets my work apart.

Based in Madrid, I work across the city and travel for weddings and editorial projects throughout Spain and beyond.
