Getting Started

How to Feed Raw in 3 Easy Steps

1

Keep Frozen

Store your Bones & Co. food in the freezer until you're ready to thaw. Our recipes are made without preservatives — the freezer is nature's pantry.

2

Thaw Safely

Move to the refrigerator up to 3 days before feeding. Thaw under refrigeration only — never on the counter or in the microwave.

3

Serve & Enjoy

Follow safe handling guidelines, serve the appropriate portion for your dog's weight, and watch them thrive. It's that simple.
