Who I am

Hi, I'm Britta — lifelong cat lover 🐈‍⬛

After a long career in graphic design, I now work from home in Hamburg-Hoheluft — which means I'm always available to visit your cats, no matter the time of day.

I've lived with cats my whole life, so I know their needs inside out: feeding, grooming, play, spotting early signs of illness, and giving them the quality time they deserve.

I also water your plants, empty the mailbox, open windows to air out the space, and handle small household tasks — a true all-round care package so you can focus on your trip without a single worry.
