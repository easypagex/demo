Cat Care Tips

Little things that make a big difference

1

Keep routines consistent

Cats are creatures of habit. Feeding, play, and sleep at similar times every day reduces stress significantly.

2

One litter box per cat — plus one

The golden rule. More boxes = fewer accidents = happier cat and happier you.

3

Hydration is key

Many cats prefer running water. A pet fountain can dramatically increase daily water intake and support kidney health.

4

Play every single day

Even 10–15 minutes of interactive play burns energy, reduces anxiety, and deepens your bond.

5

Introduce change slowly

New food, new furniture, new humans — always gradual. Cats need time to process and accept changes on their own terms.

6

Vet visits aren't optional

Annual check-ups catch problems early. Prevention is always cheaper — and kinder — than treatment.
