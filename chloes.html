What we do

Every visit is built around one thing: your cat feeling completely at home.

🚪

Drop-in visits

A quick or extended visit to feed, play, scoop the litter box, and give your cat some quality attention during your workday or short trip.

🌙

Overnight stays

We stay the night so your cat has company from bedtime to breakfast — perfect for longer trips or anxious kitties who don't love being alone.

💊

Medication & special care

Comfortable giving pills, insulin shots, and following any routine your vet has set up. Senior cats and special needs are always welcome.

📸

Photo & video updates

You'll get cute photos and videos after every visit, so you can see exactly how your cat is doing while you're away.

🪴

Home check-ins

We'll water plants, bring in mail, rotate lights, and keep an eye on things — your cat and your home, both looked after.

🧶

Playtime & enrichment

Wand toys, puzzle feeders, and plenty of zoomies. We keep things fun so your cat stays happy, active, and out of mischief.
