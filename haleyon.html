Who we are

Local, hands-on pet care across the coast — from Marbella to Sotogrande.

Halcyon Pet Services was built around one simple idea: dogs are happiest with familiar faces, fresh air and a routine they can rely on. Whether your dog needs a daily group adventure, a quiet one-to-one stroll, a day of supervised play, a wash before guests arrive, or a calm ride to the vet or across the border, our small, fully insured and first-aid trained team is on hand to help.

What we do

Five ways we look after your dog

Every service is built around your dog's routine, temperament and energy level — not the other way around.

Group dog walks

Small, well-matched packs explore the beach, paseo and countryside together — socialising, sniffing and burning energy under close supervision.

Individual walks

One-to-one time for shy, senior, reactive or simply VIP dogs who do best with a walker — and a pace — all to themselves.

Exclusive daycare

A secure, supervised space for your dog to play, rest and socialise for the day — perfect for busy schedules or travel days.

Grooming

Baths, brush-outs and tidy-ups that keep your dog fresh, comfortable and ready for the Spanish sun.

Pet taxi & European transport

Calm, safe rides to the vet, groomer, airport, or across Europe — for relocations, holidays or vet visits abroad.

Fully insured, first aid ready

Every walk and stay is covered, and every team member is trained in pet first aid — so you can relax, wherever you are.
