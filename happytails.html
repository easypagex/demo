Our Story

Where Every Pup is a VIP 👑

Happy Tails is Marbella's favourite dog boutique, nestled in the glamorous heart of Puerto Banús. We believe every dog deserves to be spoiled — from luxury grooming to the chicest accessories on the coast.

Whether you're visiting for the season or living la vida local, your four-legged companion will leave wagging their tail even harder than when they arrived.
