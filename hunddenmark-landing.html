What materials are HUND collars made from? +
We use all-natural Italian Nappa leather without unnecessary chemicals, making our collars hypoallergenic and perfect for preventing irritation and itching — even for dogs with the most sensitive skin.
Where are products made and shipped from? +
Our dog gear is crafted by skilled leather artisans in Europe. Non-custom products ship from our office near Dallas, Texas (2–3 business day USPS delivery). Custom harnesses ship from Europe and may take 2–6 weeks.
What is your return policy? +
We offer a 30-day hassle-free return and exchange window, with free return labels provided for exchanges. Initiate a return at hunddenmark.com/apps/redo/returns-portal/login.
Are your products safe for dogs with sensitive skin? +
Yes. Our collars are specifically designed to be gentle on sensitive skin, reducing the risk of irritation and discomfort. The inverted round seam and natural leather eliminate the friction points that cause most matting and hot spots.
