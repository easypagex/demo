About

Who is Iris?

Iris Bellve is a healer, teacher, and ritual guide based in New Zealand. Her work lives at the intersection of ancient wisdom and embodied practice — weaving energy work, sacred ceremony, and mindful movement into experiences that invite you to pause, feel, and return to yourself.

Whether you're arriving to a one-on-one session, a candlelit sound bath, or a moon gathering, Iris creates spaces where transformation happens gently and deeply.
