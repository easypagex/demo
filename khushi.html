Who is Khushi?

Your Guide to Body, Mind & Spirit

Khushi Bajwa is a certified yoga therapist holding an MSc in Yoga Therapy and a trained Ayurvedic Practitioner. Her practice blends the discipline of Ashtanga yoga with the healing arts of Reiki — creating a holistic experience that goes far beyond the mat.

With her compassionate guidance, students explore flexibility, mindfulness, and deep self-awareness — whether they're beginners or seasoned practitioners.
