Meet Your Cat's New Favorite Person

Kitty City Cat Sitter is a Portland-based in-home cat sitting service. We know that cats do best in their own territory — surrounded by their favorite napping spots, scratching posts, and smells.

Whether you're heading out for a weekend trip or a long vacation, we'll pop by to feed, play, scoop, and snuggle (if they're feeling social) so your kitty's routine stays exactly as they like it.
