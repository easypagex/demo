Travel by pet sitting, told one cat at a time

I trade my time and care for a place to stay — looking after cats (and their humans' homes) while exploring new cities, countries, and the occasional unexpected friendship with a grumpy tabby.

On the page, you'll find honest stories about life on the road, practical advice for anyone curious about becoming a pet sitter themselves, and far too many photos of cats sitting in suitcases.
