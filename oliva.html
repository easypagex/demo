About Oliva

Moving, feeling, becoming

I am a yoga teacher, somatic movement guide, and ceremony host based in Madrid — available wherever life calls me. My practice bridges ancient wisdom and embodied science: helping people arrive in their bodies, not just their minds.

Each session, workshop, and retreat is an invitation to slow down, tune in, and remember what you already know — deep in your cells.
