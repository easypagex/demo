Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Ultimate Pet Care?
We are a nationwide mobile grooming parlour, bringing the BEST care to man's BEST friend across South Africa since 2012.
Do you come to me?
Yes! We offer our full grooming service at the comfort of your own home. No stress for you or your pet.
What does your service include?
Bath, blow dry, brush, breed-specific cut, nail clipping, ear and teeth cleaning, hygiene cut, and our Ultimate Pet Perfume.
How often should my pet be groomed?
We recommend your pet be groomed twice a month for a healthy coat and optimal hygiene.
When was UPC established?
We started in 2012 and we are still going strong! Over a decade of professional pet grooming.
Can I become a Franchisee?
Absolutely! We would be thrilled to have you as part of the UPC family. Learn more here.
What is UPC's Vision?
To serve as exclusive hands in this busy life of our Pet Owners — making professional care convenient and stress-free.
How do I book?
Call us on 010 021 0222 or book online. It's quick and easy!
