Our Story

Rhode Island Roots,
Made for Every Dog

Located just outside Providence, Rhode Island, Up Country has been crafting premium pet products since 1984. We believe your pets deserve the finest — so every collar, lead, and harness is designed with both style and durability in mind.

Each in-house design is printed on durable webbing and paired with quality hardware built to last a lifetime. From working farms to city walks, Up Country is for all dogs who crave adventure.
