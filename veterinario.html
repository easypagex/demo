Our Practice

Where your pet
comes first

Welcome to Vet Care Vera Plaza — conveniently located near the Consum supermarket in Vera Playa, with ample parking including an emergency space (yellow cross).

We combine the latest technology with traditional veterinary techniques, in a soothing environment designed to minimise waiting and stress for your pets. We operate by appointment to keep our waiting area calm.

We are proud to be a cat-friendly practice with separated areas for dogs and cats throughout the clinic.
